Schroedinger's Equation

The behavior of a particle confined to a 1-dimensional potential well can be determined by solving Schroedinger's Equation, a transcendental function. By doing so, we can find all possible energy levels (i.e, their quantized states), their corresponding wave functions, and compare our experimental results to what is expected theoretically for a particle confined to an infinite potential well.
