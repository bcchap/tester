Schroedinger's Equation

The behavior of a confined particle can determined by solving Schroedinger's Equation. Schrodginer's Equation for a particle in a finite 1-dimensional rectangular potential is a trasncendental function, and thus cannot be solved analytically. By solving it, we can find all possible energy levels (i.e, their quantized states), their corresponding wave functions, and compare our experimental results to what is expected theoretically using the case of a particle confined to an infinite potential well.
